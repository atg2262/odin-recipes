Kimchi Mac and Cheese

Description

This dish combines two of the better known comfort foods into one melody. That would be, both kimchi and mac and cheese.

Kimchi is a fermented cabbage, with a bit of a kick from red pepper paste. Combining the acidic, spicy flavors of kimchi with creamy, melt in your mouth mac and cheese is a match made in heaven.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package elbow macaroni
  • 6 tablespoons butter, divided
  • 1.5 cups panko bread crumbs
  • 6 cups chopped kimchi, squeezed dry
  • 5.5 cubes whole wilk
  • 1.5 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 pound grated Cheddar cheese
  • 1.5 pound shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • salt and ground pepper to taste
