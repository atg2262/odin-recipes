Vanilla Ice Cream

Description

Ice cream is always a childhood favorite desert, and now you can make it at home!

All you have to do is follow this simple instructions, and you'll have a delicious treat in no time.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • 2 cups half-and-half cream
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
